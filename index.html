About Me

I'm Mohammad Sharique, a Computer Science student with a strong passion for building software that solves real-world problems. I specialize in Android development and modern web technologies, with hands-on experience in Java, React, Firebase, and algorithm design.

Over the past few years I've built projects ranging from a real-time health monitoring Android app integrated with Google Fit API, to a railway network optimizer using Dijkstra's algorithm, to a full compiler simulator built with Lex and Yacc. Each project pushed me to think deeper about system design, performance, and user experience.

Beyond coding, I've actively developed my professional skills through coursework in communication, public speaking, group discussions, and interview preparation. I believe great software is built not just with technical skill, but with clear thinking and effective communication.

I'm currently seeking internship and placement opportunities where I can contribute meaningfully, keep learning, and grow as a developer. If you have an exciting project or opportunity, I'd love to connect.
